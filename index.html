Our Advanced Delivery Technologies

Advanced
Delivery
Technologies

At Bakunova, we leverage cutting-edge delivery technologies to encapsulate, protect, and efficiently deliver active ingredients where they are needed most.

Liposomes

Biocompatible vesicles for efficient encapsulation & targeted delivery.

Microsponge Systems

Highly porous structures for controlled release & enhanced active loading.

Microemulsions

Thermodynamically stable systems for enhanced solubilization & penetration.

Ionic Liquid (CAGE)

Innovative CAGE technology for superior solubility & skin delivery.

Solid Lipid Nanoparticles

Stable lipid-based carriers for controlled release & improved skin retention.

β-CD Complexation

Improves solubility, stability & bioavailability of poorly soluble actives.

Targeted Delivery

Advanced systems ensure actives reach the target site with precision and efficiency.

Enhanced Performance

Improved stability, bioavailability, and skin retention for superior results.

Sustained Release

Controlled release mechanisms for prolonged efficacy and lasting benefits.
